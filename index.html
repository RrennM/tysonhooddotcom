Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest...

Some of my favorite time is spent in our beautiful outdoors, seeing what this world has to offer. I was fortunate to have a computer as a kid and my interest in them only grew from the first time I flipped on the power. The media fueled my interest to code and before I knew it, I was learning various forms of computer science and programming.

Now I'm a web designer. I love to solve the puzzles of great web/app design and enjoy turning even the wildest ideas into an amazing reality. Using my skills, knowledge, and experience from both in and out of the office, my goal is to create a unique and personal product which truly fits you and your needs, no matter the idea.
